Cookies Policy

This page explains how cookies and local storage are used in CHRIS Portal.

Session Storage: We store login session and app preferences to keep you signed in and improve usability.

Functional Data: Dashboard records such as leaves, training logs, and profile settings are stored for portal operation.

No Marketing Cookies: This portal does not use advertising trackers for third-party marketing.

Managing Data: You can clear browser data, but this may remove local records and require signing in again.
